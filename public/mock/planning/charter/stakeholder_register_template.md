## Stakeholder Engagement Strategies

### Strategy by Stakeholder Type

#### Executive Stakeholders

-   **Approach:** High-level strategic updates
-   **Focus:** Business value, ROI, strategic alignment
-   **Communication:** Executive summaries, dashboards
-   **Cadence:** Weekly/Monthly

#### Management Stakeholders

-   **Approach:** Operational details and progress
-   **Focus:** Milestones, risks, resource needs

-   **Approach:** Technical deep dives

-   **Communication:** Technical meetings, documentation
-   **Cadence:** Weekly

#### End User Stakeholders

-   **Approach:** Demos and feedback sessions
-   **Focus:** Usability, features, training
-   **Communication:** Workshops, demos, surveys
-   **Cadence:** Monthly/Quarterly
